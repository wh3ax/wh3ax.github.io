List of online safety tips
Topic Category Description Recommended actions Resources
Parental Controls Parental Use built-in parental controls on consoles, PCs and mobile devices to limit play time and content. Set age-appropriate restrictions, enable content filters, and schedule play hours. Use platform guides (Xbox/PlayStation/Nintendo, Epic). Guide
Account Privacy (Roblox / Fortnite) Gaming Gaming accounts often default to social and friend-based features that expose children to strangers. Set profiles to private, restrict who can message or join games, and disable friend requests from unknown users. Roblox privacy
Voice & Text Chat Safety (Discord, Fortnite) Chat Voice and text chat can expose children to abusive language, strangers, or grooming attempts. Disable voice/text chat for young accounts, use parental controls to limit who can contact your child, and teach them to leave uncomfortable conversations and report users. Discord safety
In‑App Purchases & Microtransactions Payments Games often include purchases that can lead to accidental spending or pressure to buy. Require a parent password or approval for purchases, disable saved payment methods, and review receipts regularly. Consumer tips
Stranger Interaction Social Players may encounter strangers who ask personal questions or try to move conversations off-platform. Teach children not to share personal details (full name, address, school), to block and report suspicious users, and to get a parent's help if pressured. NetSmartzKids
Screen Time & Balance Parental Excessive play can affect sleep, schoolwork, and social development. Agree on daily/weekly limits, encourage breaks, and keep devices in shared family spaces during younger ages. Screen time
Report & Safety Tools Safety Most platforms provide in-app reporting, block features, and safety centers for parents and kids. Familiarize yourself with reporting flows on platforms you use (Roblox, Discord, Epic), save incident details, and escalate to platform support when needed. Safer Internet
Account Security for Children Accounts Children's accounts can be targeted or hijacked; recovery can be difficult without parental contact info. Use strong, unique passwords, enable two-factor auth where available, and register recovery email/phone under a parent's account. Account security
Moderation & Community Standards Community Understand each game's moderation level — some communities are heavily moderated while others are not. Choose servers, games, and communities with active moderation, and review community rules before joining. Community guides
Location & Camera Sharing Privacy Sharing photos or live-streaming can reveal location or identifying details unintentionally. Disable location sharing, avoid posting images showing identifiable places or school uniforms, and review privacy for streaming platforms. Childnet
